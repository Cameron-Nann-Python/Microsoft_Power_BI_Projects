## Overview

Adventure Works is a fictional company that specializes in international orders for various products. The executives have requested a Power BI report that streamlines data insights between their customer data and bike sales. This report showcases key performance indicators and data relationships from January to April in 2023. 

## Data Insights

### Product Table

The table visual showcases products and their order totals. From the table visual, it can be seen that Adventure Works generated $173,700 in orders.

<img width="628" height="700" alt="product_order_table" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e3b1a2be-780c-417c-9f56-c959f298f4b8" />

### Product Category Chart

The column chart lists the product categories by order total in ascending order. The top performing bikes are "Mountain Bikes" at an order total of $63,500 and the lowest performing bikes are "Kid Bikes" at an order total of $500.

<img width="1361" height="752" alt="product_category_chart" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f0285f74-8f0d-4471-8501-99a20b761d7c" />

### Order Total Graph

The line chart shows the order total by day with a seasonal forecast at a 99% confidence interval. The chart predicts a general decrease in sales with an upper bound of $17,577 and a  lower bound of $17,570.

<img width="1267" height="756" alt="order_total_line_graph" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/be2b9e48-fcc7-47c3-bd1a-f5a7544469fe" />

### KPIs

The three KPIs track order total by order date, customer count by order date, and country count by order date. The KPIs show that $6,800 was the order total for 48 customers from 14 distinct countries.

<img width="903" height="117" alt="order_total_customer_country_kpis" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ca582fa5-7eb2-492a-8efb-b83f6b8b2e36" />

### QA Visual

The QA visual serves as a way to generate insights instantaneously. An example question that the QA could answer is "which product category has the highest average order quantity?" The QA visual responds with BMX bikes with an average order quantity of 2.
